# Deployment

Deploy to Vercel.

Required environment variables:

- `NEXT_PUBLIC_STORE_MODE`
- `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL`
- `CONTACT_EMAIL` (optional until configured)
- Stripe keys and webhook secret for payments
- `RESEND_API_KEY` and `EMAIL_FROM` for production emails

Keep `NEXT_PUBLIC_STORE_MODE=demo` until the launch checklist is complete.
